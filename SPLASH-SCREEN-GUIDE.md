## üé¨ Recommended Media Types

### Option 1: Animated GIF (Simplest)
- **File**: `splash-animation.gif`
- **Size**: 800x800px or 1000x1000px (square)
- **Duration**: 2-4 seconds
- **File Size**: Keep under 2MB for fast loading
- **Best For**: Simple logo animations, spinning effects

### Option 2: Video (Most Professional)
- **File**: `splash-animation.mp4` or `splash-animation.webm`
- **Size**: 1920x1080px (landscape) or 1080x1920px (portrait)
- **Duration**: 2-5 seconds
- **Codec**: H.264 (MP4) or VP9 (WebM)
- **Best For**: Complex animations, brand reveals

### Option 3: Lottie JSON (Best Performance)
- **File**: `splash-animation.json`
- **Created With**: Adobe After Effects + Bodymovin plugin
- **Best For**: Lightweight, scalable animations
- **File Size**: Usually under 100KB
